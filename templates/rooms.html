Dream Casa Homestay

About The Homestay

Garden Dreamcasa offers an affordable tea mountain view stay with cozy balcony rooms, perfect for nature lovers and peace seekers. Guests can enjoy hot and spicy homemade food, a campfire under the stars, and explore the local beauty with our sightseeing taxi services.

Convenient pickup and drop services are available from Cochin Airport to Alleppey and Thekkady with all types of vehicles at budget-friendly rates. The property includes a terrace, free private parking, and high-speed WiFi to ensure a comfortable stay.

Towels and bed linens are provided for each guest. A 24-hour front desk and full-day security ensure your safety and comfort. Wake up to breathtaking tea mountain views and enjoy a delicious à la carte or continental breakfast every morning.
